What We Do

Reaching the populations research has always struggled to reach

Fieldwork Solutions LLC develops voice-based outbound telephone platforms that deliver structured research surveys and healthcare assessments to adults aged 55 and older. Our platform requires no smartphone, no app, and no digital literacy -- just a phone call.

01

Aging Research

Purpose-built survey delivery for federally funded longitudinal aging studies. Structured instruments delivered conversationally via standard telephone.

02

Healthcare Outreach

Medicare Annual Wellness Visit outreach for primary care practices and healthcare organizations. Compliant, efficient, and accessible.

03

Disability Research

Barrier-free research participation for adults with disabilities. Outbound telephone delivery removes mobility, transportation, and access constraints entirely.
